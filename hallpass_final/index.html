About Hall Pass

Hall Pass, a web app developed by Casey Pye, was created for BYU-I's CIT 261 JavaScript course.

Hall Pass allows a classroom teacher to keep track of students who have left the room. A student checks out a card, which will turn colors with time. When the card is red, the student is late.
